# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
- **Concise & Direct:** Minimalist, high-signal, and extremely concise. Focus on clarity and efficiency in all communications.

## Branding & Visual Identity
- **Corporate & Trustworthy:** Maintain a professional and clean aesthetic suitable for enterprise environments.
- **Modern & Vibrant:** Use dynamic and innovative visual elements to reflect state-of-the-art technology.
- **Utility & Performance:** Prioritize function-over-form, ensuring the interface is efficient and data-dense.

## UX Principles
- **Accessibility-First (WCAG):** Ensure the UI is usable by everyone, strictly adhering to WCAG standards.
- **Efficiency for Power Users:** Optimize workflows for high-frequency tasks and advanced users.
- **Intuitive Onboarding:** Provide clear, immediate feedback and guidance to help new users become productive quickly.

## Documentation Style
- **Decision-Focused:** Emphasize the "Why" behind architectural choices and design decisions to provide deep context for developers.
